# orb + orb-compiler — cut of 2026-07-15 (honest snapshot, NOT fully ready)

A frozen snapshot of the verified network engine (`orb/`) and its verified-codegen compiler
(`orb-compiler/`), taken from the **last-verified committed state** — not a shippable release.
Cut deliberately *before* the engine is fully usable, and labeled as such. Made after the build
box (hbox) crashed under load; the cut is from the Mac-side committed sources, so the crash blocks
nothing here — and it is honest to freeze the *verified* state and leave the unverified in-flight
work out.

## What is IN PROGRESS (the compiler pinnacle — real but not deployed-default)
- **The translator:** a proven Lean→Pancake pipeline. `StageProg` DSL + `denote` (= the deployed
  serve, proven), and a **genuine per-constructor compiler** (by induction, not a copy-stub). Real
  responses proven byte-identical to spec; a flag-gated (`DRORB_CAKE_SERVE`) path serves cake-compiled
  machine code in the dataplane. The byte-content wiring converges on datacake.
- **datacake:** our fork of CakeML's Pancake frontend. Extension 1 (`Div/Mod`) proven green, 0-cheat,
  computing (x86_64). Codex is deputized to bring it to excellence (packed bytes → StageProg-native →
  mechanical sympathy → verified SIMD intrinsics). See `orb-compiler/datacake/`.

## What is NOT here yet (stated plainly, not prosed over)
- **The deployed engine runs via leanc** (trusted codegen). The parser and serve are *verified Lean*
  but leanc-compiled — **not yet datacake-compiled**. "Fully dynamic through datacake" is the
  destination, serve-first, then the parser. We are not there.
- **TCB:** leanc's codegen is trusted today; datacake exists to remove it. The WS DEFLATE codec
  (flate2) and the FFI/loader contracts are named trusted surfaces.
- SIMD not started. QUIC H3 real-client not yet completed. Ledger breadth partial (the dense-arm
  un-inert follow-on is in the crashed-and-unverified batch).
